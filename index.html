3D Prints Portfolio

Mechanical parts design and manufacturing samples.


Alongside a creative mindset, there’s something deeply satisfying (almost ASMR-like) about:

  • recovering broken components and restoring them to working condition
  • giving devices a second life instead of discarding them for minor faults
  • building durable alternatives rather than sacrificing quality for material-saving “optimizations”.

Being happy with...
  • PETG: for durability and stiffness
  • PC Blend: for heat resistance, toughness as well as great surface finish
  • TPU: for controlled flexibility and durability
  • TPE: for better grip alongside flexibility
  • PLA: for indoor use and aesthetic reasons
